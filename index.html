About Me

I am Rakesh Raj, and I come from Salem. I am 21 years old. I come from a small family. My brother is a painter. I studied at Vethathiri Maharishi Matric Higher Secondary School in Tharamangalam, Salem district. After my school education, I joined the Accident Emergency Care Technology (AECT) program at Vivekanandha Educational Institutions. Unfortunately, due to a lack of financial support, I had to discontinue my college degree. To support myself, I worked at a paint company. I was looking for good opportunities to uplift my life, and I applied to Freshworks STS Software Academy and cleared the exam. My hobby is playing kabaddi, and I have won many tournaments in the sport. My favorite food is chapati, and I prefer eating it every day.
